This is a matching game. Your goal is to find all the matching pairs to 8 different pairs. Once you find all the pairs, you win the game! Once you click on a card, you cannot unflip it; you can only have 2 cards flipped over at most, and if a third card is flipped, the other cards are unflipped.
Everytime you flip a card, the score will increment by 1, meaning that the lowest (or best) score you can get is 16.
Extra: You can zoom in and zoom out to make the cards fit better.
