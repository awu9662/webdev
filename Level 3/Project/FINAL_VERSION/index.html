Rules

This is Rock Paper Scissors (Xtreme Edition).

You play against a computer and start with the same number of "cards." The game is simple: you choose one of three cards (rock, paper, or scissors).

The winner is determined by the following: paper > rock > scissors > paper.

The winner of each round obtains the opponent's card and gets back their own.

A player wins the entire match if the opponent has no cards remaining.

Additional rules:

If a player plays the same card three times in a row, that card will "break," meaning that one card will be removed (only that one card, not the entire type of card).

Builders

If you toggle on Build Mode, you can select two cards to trade for the other one (i.e. You can trade rock and scissors in exchange for paper).

The two selected cards will be deducted, while the other card will be increased (both by 1).

The computer will also build if it runs out of a certain type of card. Its cards numbers will be highlighted when it does so to show what happened.

Intelligent Computer

If this optional gameplay enhancement is enabled, the computer will play "smart", meaning it will try avoid breaking cards and playing cards you don't have.

The computer will only build cards if this option is enabled.
