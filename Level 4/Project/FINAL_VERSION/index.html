How to Play

Tic Tac Toe, with a twist.

Basically the same as regular Tic Tac Toe, but you can take squares away from the other player.
Play the same square twice to claim it for good.

To take over an opposing square, just click on that square.
You can only take it over if that square wasn't taken last turn by the opponent.
X always goes first.

When "Human" Computer is checked, the computer will try to make the best move based on the current board.
