How to Play

Basics

- Start with a shuffled 52-card deck

- Everytime you draw a card, you have to place it somewhere on the 4x4 board (cards are drawn automatically)

- Kings, Queens, and Jacks can only be placed on their respective spots, as shown on the board
(Kings in the corner, Queens in the left and right, Jacks in the top and bottom)

- Other cards can be placed anywhere, including the royalty spots

- Once the entire board is filled, removal phase starts

Removal Phase

- Removal phase is indicated when no card is drawn

- Select a 10 or two cards that add up to 10 to remove them from the board (aces = 1)

- Once all possible cards are removed, continue drawing and placing cards

Winning

- Drawing cards, placing cards, and removal phase all repeat until the game is lost or won

- The game is won when all the picture cards are in the correct spot and the center of the board is empty

- The game is lost when a card can't be placed or removed

How to use cheats?

Turning this on enables a button on the board that lets you burn cards.
When that button is on, click on the discard pile (red card back) to burn the current card - can't burn picture cards.

Use at your own risk. Cheats may break the game (probably not).
